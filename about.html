📱 Install as App (PWA)

On Android (Chrome / Edge):
1. Open STREAMVAULT in your browser.
2. Tap the menu (three dots) → "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
3. Confirm and launch from your home screen.

On iOS (Safari):
1. Open the site in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen".
3. Name it and tap "Add".

After installation, you'll enjoy a full‑screen app experience with offline fallback.

🔧 How to Use

  • Click any channel card to start streaming.
  • Use the search bar to find a channel instantly.
  • Filter by category (Entertainment, News, Sports, etc.) using the buttons above the grid.
  • Click the heart icon on any channel to add to favorites – your list is saved automatically.
  • Recently watched channels appear at the top for quick access.
  • If a stream stalls, use the Retry button to reload.
  • Network speed is shown in real time; a green dot indicates good connection, yellow/orange means moderate, red indicates slow.
